# CityPlay : Privacy Policy

Welcome to the CityPlay app!

CityPlay is available on Google Play and the App Store. CityPlay is a loyalty application that allows users to manage their account and track points, loyalty cards, and rewards. Login is required to use the main features of the Application.

As users, we take privacy very seriously. We know how irritating it is when apps collect your data without your knowledge.

What data CityPlay collects

CityPlay collects only the minimum information needed to make the app work properly, such as:

Account information (for example: username, and possibly email/phone depending on your registration)

CityPlay does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Camera permission

CityPlay uses the camera permission only to scan QR codes.
CityPlay does not record videos and does not store camera images.

Files / Photos permission

CityPlay may request access to your files/photos only if you choose to upload a profile photo.
This access is used only to select the image you want to upload.

Notifications

CityPlay may send you notifications (for example: updates, rewards, or offers) only if you allow notifications in your device settings. You can disable notifications at any time.
